Event & Conference Management Department

“Precision in Planning. Excellence in Execution.”

At SALMART Diplomatic Hospitality, the Event & Conference Management Department serves as the strategic engine behind high-level gatherings that influence diplomacy, business, science, and development across Africa and beyond.

We curate and deliver seamless, content-driven events with measurable outcomes, working in synergy with other departments without overlapping roles.

Vision

To be Africa’s leading authority in designing and executing high-impact, content-driven conferences and diplomatic forums that drive change and elevate engagement.

Mission

To deliver world-class event and conference management services by combining strategic planning, protocol understanding, and operational precision to ensure every gathering achieves its diplomatic, policy, or commercial objectives.

Strategic Objectives

  1. Curate High-Impact Agendas aligned with client vision, regional priorities, and global trends.
  2. Ensure Seamless Event Execution through agile coordination, real-time management, and professional moderation.
  3. Maintain Diplomatic and Technical Excellence across all aspects of event programming, without duplicating protocol or logistical roles.
  4. Foster Strategic Dialogue by engaging expert speakers, thought leaders, and institutional actors.
  5. Deliver Monitoring and Evaluation for every conference, with post-event reports, key takeaways, and measurable outcomes.

Core Services

1. Concept Development & Agenda Structuring

  • Event theme, flow, and objective development
  • Background note drafting and stakeholder mapping
  • Integration of cross-sectoral, gender-responsive, and policy-relevant themes

2. Speaker & Panel Management

  • Identification, invitation, and preparation of speakers and moderators
  • Coordination of bios, technical briefs, and session roles
  • Speaker support, presentation alignment, and VIP facilitation

3. Program Delivery & Onsite Oversight

  • Live event coordination, stage direction, and session flow management
  • Agenda timekeeping, transitions, and troubleshooting
  • Backstage and green room management for panelists and dignitaries

4. Monitoring & Real-Time Quality Control

  • Session evaluation, time tracking, and feedback loops
  • Mid-event adjustments and flow optimization
  • Resolution of programming gaps, cancellations, or overlaps

5. Post-Event Documentation & Reporting

  • Recruitment and management of professional rapporteurs
  • Preparation of communiqués, event reports, and outcome documents
  • Knowledge product creation for policy advocacy or donor reporting

Our Events Include

  • Government Summits & Ministerial Retreats
  • Business Forums, CEO Roundtables & Investment Pitch Events
  • Science-Policy Dialogues & Technical Workshops
  • Intergovernmental, AU, UN, and regional development conferences
  • Hybrid & Virtual Events with real-time coordination

Collaborative Yet Distinct

This department collaborates with:

  • The Protocol & Diplomatic Services Department for guest protocols and government liaison
  • The Destination Management & Hospitality Department for logistical execution
  • The Media & Communications Department for branding and live coverage
