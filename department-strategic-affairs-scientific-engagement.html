Strategic Affairs & Scientific Engagement Department

“Strategy, Partnerships, and Knowledge for High-Impact Engagement”

This department manages partnerships, stakeholder engagement, policy dialogue, and participation in scientific and innovation platforms—supporting institutions and delegations where strategy and credibility matter.

We help organizations navigate complex ecosystems, align technical narratives with public and diplomatic priorities, and convene the right actors to move decisions forward.

Mission

To provide strategic advisory, stakeholder coordination, and knowledge-driven engagement that strengthens cooperation, informs decisions, and advances institutional objectives.

Vision

To be a trusted regional partner for strategic affairs and scientific engagement—where diplomacy, evidence, and innovation converge to deliver meaningful outcomes.

Core Functions

1. Stakeholder Mapping & Engagement Strategy

  • Stakeholder identification across government, academia, private sector, and development partners
  • Engagement planning for high-level meetings, forums, and missions
  • Partnership pathways and relationship management support

2. Policy Dialogue & Strategic Advisory

  • Positioning support for institutions and technical programs
  • Briefs, talking points, and agenda alignment for leadership engagements
  • Facilitation of policy and investment roundtables

3. Scientific Events, Delegations & Platforms

  • Coordination for scientific delegations, visits, and exchange missions
  • Support for conferences, innovation forums, and research showcases
  • Speaker management, program design, and stakeholder convening

4. Partnerships with Research & Innovation Institutions

  • Engagement with universities, research institutions, and innovation hubs
  • Collaboration frameworks and MoU support
  • Coordination of joint initiatives and multi-stakeholder programs

5. Knowledge Products & Strategic Communication Support

  • Development of briefs, concept notes, and institutional profiles
  • Support for evidence-based narratives and stakeholder-facing materials
  • Coordination with media and production teams for publication-ready outputs

For Our Clients

We help you build the right relationships, shape credible narratives, and convene meaningful engagements—so technical initiatives gain visibility, alignment, and sustained support.

For Salmart Diplomatic

We strengthen institutional positioning by supporting strategy-led partnerships, knowledge-driven convenings, and high-trust engagement across sectors.
