Cruise Line Services

We curate exclusive cruise-based experiences for corporate and private clients seeking distinction, privacy, and seamless delivery at sea.

SALMART plans and coordinates cruise-based programs that feel effortless—aligning the itinerary, onboard experiences, and destination moments into one cohesive journey.

What We Deliver

  • Corporate conferences and meetings at sea
  • Incentive programs aboard luxury cruise liners
  • Private charter cruises for executive and VIP groups
  • Curated shore excursions and destination experiences
  • Themed cruises aligned with brand or event objectives
Operational note We design for privacy and flow: movement planning, VIP zones, event timings, and discreet coordination with onboard operations.
